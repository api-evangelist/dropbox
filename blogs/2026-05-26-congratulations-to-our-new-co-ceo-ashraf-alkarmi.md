Today, we announced that Ashraf Alkarmi will become Dropbox co-CEO alongside Drew Houston. Following a transition period, Ashraf will become sole CEO and Drew will move into the role of executive chairman. Drew and Ashraf shared the news with Dropboxers this morning in the emails below.
